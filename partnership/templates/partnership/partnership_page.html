Becoming a partner:

APN welcomes partners from different countries and ministries all over the world to support and facilitate the realization of its mission. Partnership is established by a longterm commitment to either financially support the mission, and/or volunteer skill/time for service. You can partner financially by subscribing for a periodic contribution and/or by volunteering your time and skills.
